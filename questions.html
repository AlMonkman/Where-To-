featured places

Maldives, Asia

An archipelagic country located in the Indian Ocean. This country offers picture-perfect private villas suspended over striking blue waters, alabaster white sand beaches and spectacular sunsets dipping into the horizon.

Ha Long Bay, Vietnam

Located in northern Vietnam, Ha Long Bay is a world heritage site and popular travel destination. The bay itself is populated with towering limestone pillars and tiny islets. One of the most beautiful and affordable destinations we reccomend.

Harbin City, China

With its bitterly cold winters, Harbin is heralded as the Ice City (冰城) for its well-known winter tourism and recreations. It is located in Northeast China, a wonderful choice for a winter wonderland vacation.

Bora Bora, French Polynesia

A small group of islands located in the pacific ocean. Bora Bora is a major international tourist destination for its beautiful weather and luxury resorts.

Saariselka, finland

The northern most ski resort in Finland. This location is very popular for the views of the northern lights, and it's glass igloo hotels.

Pink Lake, Australia

Australia's vast country boasts many natural wonders, but few are more beautiful than the Pink Lakes. With multiple dotted across the entire country you will have plenty to do on this vacation destination.
